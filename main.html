• Poem

    Be my morning peace and I'll be your calm in the night.
    Am no knight in shining armour
    But my heart will be your shield to protect your from the world
    and all of me will be your sword to fight the world for you
    To me you are a Goddess from descended from heaven

    Bendithi ndonele luthando
    but you fit in perfectly
    Olwam nolwakho aludalelwa lomhlaba.
    Obubuhle bakho was never nothing for man to compare
    to isihlabathi solwandle or even ilanga liphuma
    uBuhlendalo! Intombi obuso benkosazana,
    Umfazi onenqondo wakhosikazi abusa amazwe.
